Forests: Lungs of the Planet

Forests are among Earth’s most awe-inspiring ecosystems, home to ancient trees, intricate plant networks, and countless organisms that rely on these habitats for survival. These lush environments provide oxygen, stabilize climates, and serve as a sanctuary for millions of species, many still undiscovered.

Yet, forests are under pressure from deforestation, pollution, and unsustainable resource use. Understanding the importance of these ecosystems and taking responsible action—such as promoting reforestation and sustainable practices—can help preserve the natural beauty and biodiversity that forests offer to our planet.
